Physical VS Virtual Self

Artwork / Blog

This is how I depict myself in terms of real and virtual self. First I will be elaborating on my real self depiction, and that basically just shows myself as the person in the middle, surrounded by the interests I have, or the main things that I think I do well, and define me, namely: coding, photography, music, astronomy, video games, and history. These are the things that I love most doing, and most people know me or remember me through these hobbies or interests. Coding since that is the current track or course I am taking, music and photography since I have been doing that since I was a child, video games since I play a lot of it with my friends to destress and hang out, especially during the pandemic, and lastly, astronomy and history are my two best or most interested subjects that I like to study about.

Next is my virtual self, to give context, I have drawn Yuuma Isogai, he is my favorite character in an anime called “Assassination Classroom”. The reason why I have chosen to depict him as my virtual self is first of all, we share the same birthday, and next is because of his qualities. He possesses qualities that I want to have myself, like being a jack-of-all-trades, very smart in terms of academics, and very capable in physical activities if the need arises. He is very dependable, and he always stands for what is right and just, which I believe is an important quality to have. He is also very loving and caring for his family, and is a great leader, which is why he is the president of his class. He is a very well-rounded, simple, down-to-earth person who is always willing to help no matter what, and he doesn’t care about what others say as long as he is not hurting anyone, and I aspire to be like him someday.
