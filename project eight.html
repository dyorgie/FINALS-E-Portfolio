The message of the ad pertains to that bad days happen, or in terms of travelling, rainy days happen and it is a hassle. That is why they are endorsing their backpack, that says that even if ba or rainy days happen, their things or belongings would be safe and dry. The ad is mostly targeted to those who travel or hike alot, but it is available for anyone who just wants a waterproof backpack. Yes, i believe that the graphics are appropriate and clear and it goes well with the message it tries to show, and it is appropriate for all kinds of audiences. For me personally, the only thing out of place is the rain or cloud graphic. it seems out of place for me, and does not go well with the other elements present. Like previously said, i think that the rain graphic is out of place, so for me I would change that so that the ad looks more uniform and complete
